Frequently Asked Questions

How do I update my location for prayer times?

Go to the Prayer Times tab and allow location access when prompted.

Can I share my iftar photos?

Yes! During Ramadan, tap the camera icon on the Ramadan tab to share your iftar meal with the global community.

How do I track my fasting?

Visit the Ramadan tab and tap "Fasting Calendar" to see recommended fasting days throughout the year.
